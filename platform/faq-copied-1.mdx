## General

  </Accordion>

## Content Hub

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Jinmi Content Hub support English and Vietnamese?">
    Yes. Jinmi Content Hub supports both English and Vietnamese content generation. You can write prompts, generate output, and refine content in either language. This makes it well-suited for businesses operating in Vietnam and for teams targeting international English-speaking markets.

    If you need support for additional languages, contact the Jinmi team at [info@jinmi.ai](mailto:info@jinmi.ai) to discuss your requirements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can AI write long, SEO-friendly articles?">
    Yes. Content Hub includes a dedicated **Blog Writing & SEO Content** feature designed to produce long-form articles that are structured for search engine visibility. You can input a topic or keyword, and the AI will generate a complete, well-structured article with SEO best practices applied — including headings, meta descriptions, and keyword placement.

    The AI also suggests SEO-ready topics and content ideas so you can plan content that targets the right search intent from the start.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I create synthetic voices with Jinmi Content Hub?">
    Yes. Content Hub includes an **Audio Production** feature that lets you generate synthetic voiceovers from text. This is useful for creating video narration, podcast content, product demos, and marketing audio — without hiring a voice actor or recording studio.

    You can find this feature under the **Media Creation** module in Content Hub. See [Media Creation](/content-hub/media-creation) for details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is customer data secure?">
    Yes. Jinmi AI takes data security seriously. Your content, customer information, and training data are stored securely and are not shared with third parties. Access to your account data is restricted to authorized users only.

    For specific questions about data handling, privacy policies, or compliance requirements for your industry, review the [Privacy Policy](https://jinmi.ai/en/privacy) or contact the team at [info@jinmi.ai](mailto:info@jinmi.ai).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use Jinmi Content Hub for free?">
    Yes. Jinmi Content Hub is available on the free plan, which includes a set of features you can use immediately at no cost. The free plan is designed for individuals and small businesses getting started with AI content creation.

    As your needs grow — more content volume, advanced features, or team collaboration — you can upgrade to a paid plan. Visit [jinmi.ai/en/pricing](https://jinmi.ai/en/pricing) or contact the team for pricing details.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I own the rights to generated images and videos?">
    Yes. Content you generate using Jinmi Content Hub — including AI-generated images, videos, and written content — belongs to you. You can use it for commercial purposes, including marketing, advertising, and product listings.

    For specific licensing questions or to understand any edge cases, contact the team at [info@jinmi.ai](mailto:info@jinmi.ai).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is content history saved?">
    Yes. Content Hub includes a **Document** feature under **Management & Storage** that stores all your previously generated content. You can browse, search, and reuse past outputs at any time — so nothing you create is lost.

    This makes it easy to build on previous work, repurpose content across channels, and maintain a library of assets for your brand.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is it easy to use without technical skills?">
    Yes. Content Hub is built for marketers, business owners, and content creators — not developers. The interface is straightforward: you describe what you need, and the AI generates it. No coding, design skills, or technical background is required.

    If you're new to the platform, the [quickstart guide](/getting-started/quickstart) walks you through your first content creation in a few minutes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Omnichannel

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can the chatbot answer every customer question?">
    The chatbot answers questions based on the knowledge you train it with — using scripts, FAQs, and product information you upload as training files. For topics it hasn't been trained on, it can escalate the conversation to a human agent seamlessly.

    The more training data you provide, the more accurately the chatbot responds. Jinmi AI Agentic (the underlying automation platform) allows the chatbot to learn from conversations over time, improving its responses continuously. For complex or sensitive questions, you can configure the bot to always hand off to a human.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Are there limits on script length or number?">
    The number of training files you can upload depends on your plan:

    - **Free:** 3 training files
    - **Basic:** 5 training files
    - **Advanced:** 15 training files
    - **Professional:** 30 training files
    - **Enterprise:** Unlimited

    There is no strict limit on the length of individual scripts, but keeping training content clear and well-structured improves the chatbot's accuracy. Contact the Jinmi team if you have specific requirements around training data volume.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can the chatbot process orders and payments?">
    Yes. Jinmi Omnichannel supports automated order processing flows — the chatbot can collect order details, confirm purchases, and guide customers through the checkout process. Payment verification can be integrated with **Jinmi Tingbee**, which confirms bank transfers in real time and sends notifications across your channels.

    For detailed setup instructions, see [Automations](/omnichannel/automations) or contact the Jinmi team to configure this for your store.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does Jinmi Omnichannel support sales scripts?">
    Yes. You can build and upload sales scripts that the AI chatbot follows during customer conversations. Scripts can cover common sales scenarios — product inquiries, objection handling, upselling, and closing — allowing the bot to act as a consistent, always-on sales assistant.

    Scripts are uploaded as training files and can be updated at any time. The more detailed and natural your scripts are, the better the chatbot performs.
  </Accordion>

    The team can clarify what's covered under your specific plan and help resolve any billing or service concerns.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
