Text Content is the heart of Content Hub. It eliminates the two biggest pain points in any marketing workflow — idea blocks and time pressure — by giving you an AI writing partner that produces consistent, high-quality copy for every customer touchpoint.

You provide a topic, keyword, or brief. The AI produces a complete, ready-to-use draft in seconds.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Blog writing & SEO">
    ## Blog writing & SEO content

    Write long-form, search-engine-optimized articles without spending hours on research, outlining, or drafting.

    **What you can create:**
    - Full SEO blog posts with headings, body copy, and keyword placement
    - Content outlines and article structures
    - Topic ideas and content calendars
    - Meta titles and meta descriptions

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter your topic or keyword">
        Type the subject you want to cover. You can include a target keyword, a working title, or just a broad idea.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set your parameters">
        Choose your preferred article length, tone (informative, conversational, authoritative), and any specific points you want covered.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate your article">
        Content Hub produces a full draft — including an introduction, structured sections, and a conclusion — with SEO best practices built in.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and publish">
        Read through the draft, make any edits, and copy it to your CMS or blog platform.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Use the **Content Improver** in the [Content Refinement](/content-hub/content-refinement) section to polish your article after generation.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Marketing & advertising">
    ## Marketing & advertising copy

    Produce compelling ad copy and campaign messaging that drives clicks and conversions — without a copywriter on staff.

    **What you can create:**
    - Facebook and Google ad headlines and body copy
    - Campaign taglines and slogans
    - Promotional offers and sale announcements
    - Landing page copy

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Describe your campaign">
        Enter your product or offer, your target audience, and the goal of the ad (awareness, clicks, conversions).
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select your format">
        Choose the ad type — short-form for social, long-form for search, or a tagline for brand campaigns.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate multiple variations">
        Content Hub produces several copy options so you can A/B test different messages.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pick and deploy">
        Choose the version that fits your campaign and paste it directly into your ad platform.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Generate 3–5 variations of the same ad and test them against each other to find your best performer.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="E-commerce">
    ## E-commerce content

    Write product descriptions and listings that sell — consistent, accurate, and optimized for both search and conversion.

    **What you can create:**
    - Product descriptions for your website, Shopee, Lazada, or TikTok Shop
    - Short listing titles and bullet-point feature summaries
    - Category page copy
    - Upsell and cross-sell blurbs

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Enter your product details">
        Provide the product name, key features, materials, dimensions, or any other relevant specifications.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set the tone and length">
        Choose a tone (professional, enthusiastic, minimal) and the length format appropriate for your platform.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate your description">
        Content Hub writes a compelling description that highlights benefits, not just features.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Copy to your store">
        Paste the description directly into your product listing on any platform.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>
      For large product catalogs, you can generate descriptions in bulk by submitting multiple product briefs in sequence.
    </Note>
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Social, email & branding">
    ## Social, email & branding

    Maintain a consistent brand voice across every channel — from Instagram captions to email newsletters to brand messaging.

    **What you can create:**
    - Social media captions for Facebook, Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn
    - Email subject lines and newsletter body copy
    - Brand taglines and mission statements
    - Customer re-engagement and promotional emails

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose your channel">
        Select the platform or format — social post, email, or brand copy — so the AI tailors the length and tone appropriately.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Describe your message">
        Tell the AI what you want to communicate: a new product, a promotion, a brand story, or a weekly update.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set your brand voice">
        Specify your tone — friendly, professional, bold, or casual — to keep every piece on-brand.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate and schedule">
        Copy the output to your social scheduler, email platform, or brand guidelines document.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      Use the same tone settings across all four content types to build a consistent brand identity without extra effort.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
