Content Refinement takes what you already have and makes it better. Whether you need to elevate a rough draft, adapt content for a different audience, or condense a long document into key takeaways, these tools act as your always-available AI editor and writing assistant.

You can also use the Interactive AI tools in this section to build automated chatbots or get help with any open-ended writing task.

## Editing and rewriting tools

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Content Improver">
    **Elevate clarity, professionalism, and impact.**

    Content Improver takes your existing draft and rewrites it to be clearer, more compelling, and more professional — without changing your core message.

    **When to use it:**
    - Your first draft feels flat or unclear
    - You want to raise the quality of AI-generated content before publishing
    - You need to match a higher standard of writing for a premium audience

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Paste your content">
        Copy your existing draft — a paragraph, a full article, or any piece of text — into the Content Improver input field.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Run the improvement">
        Click **Improve** and the AI rewrites your content with stronger word choices, tighter sentences, and better flow.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and accept">
        Compare the improved version with your original and accept the changes you want to keep.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content Rephrase">
    **Rewrite your content in a different tone or style.**

    Content Rephrase lets you take the same message and express it differently — more formal, more casual, more persuasive, or adapted for a completely different audience or platform.

    **When to use it:**
    - Repurposing a blog post as a social caption
    - Adapting B2B copy for a consumer audience
    - Making formal documentation more approachable
    - Creating multiple versions of the same message for A/B testing

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Paste your source content">
        Enter the text you want to rephrase.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set the target tone or style">
        Specify how you want the rewrite to sound: casual, formal, persuasive, friendly, authoritative, or simplified.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate the rephrased version">
        The AI rewrites your content while preserving the original meaning.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Use or iterate">
        Accept the result or run it again with different tone settings to get another variation.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Text Summarizer">
    **Condense long content into key points.**

    Text Summarizer extracts the most important information from any piece of long-form content and presents it as a concise summary — saving your readers time and helping you repurpose detailed content for faster formats.

    **When to use it:**
    - Creating a TL;DR for a long blog post or report
    - Pulling key takeaways for a social post or email
    - Reviewing a long document quickly before editing

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Paste your long-form content">
        Copy the text you want summarized — articles, reports, transcripts, or any long document.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose your summary length">
        Select how condensed you want the output: a single sentence, a short paragraph, or a bullet-point list.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate the summary">
        The AI identifies the core message and key supporting points and presents them clearly.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Explain it to a Child">
    **Simplify complex content for any audience.**

    Explain it to a Child rewrites technical, dense, or jargon-heavy content in plain, simple language that anyone can understand — without losing the essential meaning.

    **When to use it:**
    - Making product documentation accessible to non-technical users
    - Simplifying legal, medical, or financial content for a general audience
    - Creating educational content for younger readers
    - Testing whether your own content is actually clear

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Paste your complex content">
        Enter the text you want simplified.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Generate the simplified version">
        The AI rewrites your content using everyday language, shorter sentences, and relatable analogies.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Review and adapt">
        Use the simplified version as-is or as a reference to rewrite your original in a more accessible style.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

    ## AI Chatbot builder

    Build an automated chatbot that can answer questions, guide visitors, and capture leads — powered by your own content.

    **What you can build:**
    - Customer support bots for your website
    - FAQ chatbots trained on your product documentation
    - Lead capture bots for landing pages
    - Sales assistant bots for e-commerce

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Define your chatbot's purpose">
        Decide what your chatbot should do — answer product questions, capture lead information, or guide users through a process.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add your content and training data">
        Provide the information you want the chatbot to know: FAQs, product descriptions, support scripts, or your documentation.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Configure responses and flows">
        Set up the conversation paths — how the bot greets visitors, what questions it asks, and how it responds to common queries.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Activate and embed">
        Deploy your chatbot to your website or integrate it with your messaging channels.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Note>

  <Tab title="AI Chat">
    ## AI Chat

    A versatile conversational AI assistant for any content task — brainstorming, drafting, researching, editing, or answering questions.

    **What you can use it for:**
    - Brainstorming content ideas and angles
    - Getting quick feedback on a draft
    - Researching a topic before writing
    - Writing or editing anything that doesn't fit a specific template
    - Asking questions about your own content

    **How to use it:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open AI Chat">
        Navigate to the AI Chat section in Content Hub.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Type your request">
        Ask a question, paste content for feedback, describe what you need, or start a brainstorming session. Use natural language — no special formatting required.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Iterate in the conversation">
        Follow up with refinements, ask for variations, or dig deeper into a topic. AI Chat maintains context throughout the conversation so you can build on previous responses.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Tip>
      AI Chat is the best starting point when you're not sure which specific tool to use. Describe what you're trying to accomplish and the assistant will help you get there.
    </Tip>
  </Tab>
