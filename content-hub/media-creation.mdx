## Image generation

Turn a text description into a professional visual — product shots, social banners, marketing graphics, or custom illustrations.

**What you can create:**
- Product lifestyle images and mockups
- Social media graphics and banners
- Blog post featured images
- Brand illustrations and icons

**How to use it:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write your image prompt">
    Describe the image you want: the subject, setting, style, and mood. Be as specific as you can — more detail produces better results.

    Example: *"A flat-lay of skincare products on a marble surface with soft natural lighting, minimalist style"*
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your style and dimensions">
    Choose a visual style (photorealistic, illustrated, minimal) and the aspect ratio or resolution that fits your use case — square for social, landscape for banners, portrait for product pages.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your image">
    Content Hub produces your image in seconds. You can generate multiple variations from the same prompt.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and use">
    Download your image and add it directly to your content, website, or social post.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  If the first result isn't quite right, adjust one or two words in your prompt and regenerate. Small changes — like adding "warm lighting" or "dark background" — can significantly change the output.
</Tip>

## Video production

Produce marketing videos without a camera, crew, or editing timeline. Describe your video concept and Content Hub generates a complete, shareable video.

**What you can create:**
- Product demonstration and showcase videos
- Social media short-form videos
- Brand introduction clips
- Promotional and sale announcement videos

**How to use it:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your video concept">
    Write a brief for the video: the product or message, the audience, the mood, and the call to action you want at the end.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your format">
    Select the video length and format — short-form for TikTok and Instagram Reels, horizontal for YouTube, or square for Facebook.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your video">
    Content Hub assembles your video using AI-generated visuals, motion, and structure based on your brief.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and publish">
    Download the finished video and post it directly to your social channels or embed it on your website.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Audio production

Create professional-quality voiceovers, narrations, and audio content without a microphone or recording setup.

**What you can create:**
- Voiceovers for video content
- Audio narrations for presentations and tutorials
- Podcast-style introductions
- Brand audio identities

**How to use it:**

<Steps>
  <Step title="Write your script">
    Type the text you want converted to audio. You can paste an existing script or write one from scratch — use the [Text Content](/content-hub/text-content) tools to generate a script first if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a voice style">
    Choose from a range of voice types, tones, and pacing options to match your brand and content style.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate your audio">
    Content Hub converts your script to a natural-sounding voiceover in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and add to your content">
    Download the audio file and add it to your video, presentation, or podcast.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Combine all three tools in sequence: generate an image, produce a video, and add a voiceover to create a complete, professional content asset in a single session — no external tools required.
</Tip>
