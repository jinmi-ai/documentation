Jinmi Content Hub is your AI content creation assistant. You give it a single idea, and it helps you produce a full library of high-quality assets — long-form blog posts, product descriptions, social captions, marketing videos, images, and audio — in minutes instead of days.

Whether you're a solo marketer, a small business owner, or a growing team, Content Hub removes the two biggest blockers in content creation: running out of ideas and running out of time.

## What you can do with Content Hub

- **Create a full month of content in a single morning** — generate blog posts, social captions, email campaigns, and product descriptions in one session
- **Build a consistent brand identity** — maintain the same tone and voice across every channel and content type
- **Rank on Google with SEO-ready content** — AI-suggested structure, keywords, and article formats that search engines reward
- **Produce professional visuals and video** — no design or editing skills required
- **Protect your brand before you publish** — check for plagiarism and AI-generated content before anything goes live

## How it works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start with an idea">
    Enter a topic, keyword, or brief. You can be as specific or as general as you like — Content Hub works with both.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your content type">
    Select the format you need: a blog post, a product description, a social caption, an image, a video, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI generates your content">
    Content Hub produces a complete draft in seconds. For longer formats like blog posts, it generates structure, headings, and full body copy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and refine">
    Use the built-in refinement tools to adjust tone, rephrase sections, or summarize as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish or save">
    Copy your content directly to your publishing platform, or save it to your document history for later use.
  </Step>
</Steps>
