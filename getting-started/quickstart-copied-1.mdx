Jinmi AI gives you two powerful products to start with: **Content Hub** for AI-driven content creation, and **Omnichannel** for managing customer conversations across every channel. Both are available on the free plan — no credit card required.

## Create your account

Go to [portal.jinmi.ai](https://portal.jinmi.ai/) and sign up for a free account. You'll be up and running in under a minute.

<Note>
  The free plan includes 200 messages/month, 3 training files, and 1 integrated inbox. No credit card is required to get started.
</Note>

## Get up and running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign up at portal.jinmi.ai">
    Visit [portal.jinmi.ai](https://portal.jinmi.ai/) and create your free account. No credit card is needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a product to start with">
    After logging in, select **Content Hub** or **Omnichannel** from the dashboard. You can switch between products at any time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your first channel (Omnichannel) or generate your first asset (Content Hub)">
    - **Omnichannel**: Connect your first inbox — Facebook, Zalo OA, or your website live chat. Your free plan supports 1 integrated inbox.
    - **Content Hub**: Pick a content type (blog post, social caption, product description) and enter a topic or idea. The AI generates a full draft in seconds.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Train your AI (optional but recommended)">
    Upload up to 3 training files — product catalogs, brand guidelines, or FAQ documents — so the AI learns your business context and produces more accurate, on-brand results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go live">
    - **Omnichannel**: Activate your AI chatbot to start responding to customers automatically, 24/7.
    - **Content Hub**: Review your generated assets, refine them with the Content Improver or Rephrase tools, and publish.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Start with the product that solves your most pressing problem today. If you're overwhelmed with customer messages, begin with Omnichannel. If you're struggling to produce content consistently, start with Content Hub.
</Tip>
