## **Part 1: ACCOUNT REGISTRATION/LOGIN**

### **1. Create an account**

Step 1: On the registration screen → select **_Continue with Google_**

Step 2: Log in with your Google account

Step 3: Set up your store information → Click **_Continue_**

You have now completed registration for Jinmi Omnichannel.

### **2. Log in**

There are 2 ways to log in to the Omni platform:

- <u>Method 1: Log in with your account</u>

Step 1: Go to: [<u>Jinmi Portal</u>](https://portal.jinmi.ai/auth?return_url=https%3A%2F%2Fportal.jinmi.ai%2Fapi%2Foauth%2Fauthorize%3Fclient_id%3Domnichannel_client_id%26redirect_uri%3Dhttps%253A%252F%252Fomni.jinmi.ai%252Fportal%252Fsso%252Fcallback%26response_type%3Dcode%26scope%3Dopenid%2Bprofile%2Bemail%26state%3DLw%253D%253D)

Step 2: Enter your registered email and a valid password, then click "Log in"

\

- <u>Method 2: Log in with Google</u>

Step 1: On the login screen, click "Log in with Google"

Step 2: Select the account you want to log in with

You have now successfully logged in to the Jinmi Omnichannel system.
