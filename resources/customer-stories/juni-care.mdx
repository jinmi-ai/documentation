JUNI Care General Clinic is a healthcare and wellness facility that serves hundreds of patients every day through Facebook, Zalo, its website, and a direct hotline. Before Jinmi AI, the clinic was operating on manual processes that couldn't scale — and it was costing them both patients and money.

## Background

Healthcare requires fast, accurate, and compassionate communication. Patients reaching out to schedule appointments, ask pre-treatment questions, or follow up on their care don't tolerate long waits. JUNI Care understood this — but their systems couldn't keep up with demand.

Staff managed multiple channels simultaneously with no unified dashboard. Repetitive tasks — appointment confirmations, follow-up reminders, medication guidance — consumed hours of every working day. To cope with the load, the clinic increased its customer service headcount by 30%. Costs rose. Results didn't match expectations.

## Challenges

### Message overload caused patient delays

Every day, hundreds of messages arrived across Facebook, Zalo, the clinic website, and the hotline. The **average response time was 2 hours** — and patients lost patience. Statistics showed that **up to 30% of patients left due to delayed responses**, directly damaging both reputation and revenue.

### Fragmented channels with no reporting

Each communication channel operated in isolation. The clinic owner had no visibility into which channels were driving results, how individual staff were performing, or what the actual conversion rates looked like. Every decision relied on intuition.

### High personnel costs with low efficiency

The 30% increase in customer service headcount didn't solve the root problem. Appointment reminder errors and data entry mistakes remained high. The clinic was spending more while delivering inconsistent results.

## Solution: Jinmi AI

JUNI Care implemented Jinmi AI across all its patient communication channels, building out a structured digital transformation with three core components.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Multi-channel integration and 24/7 automated responses">
    All messages from Facebook, Zalo, and the website were consolidated into a single management interface. The AI chatbot automatically answered frequently asked questions — about services, pricing, and availability — while complex or sensitive cases were immediately routed to clinical staff. Patients received a response at any hour, including evenings and weekends.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Personalised patient care scenarios">
    The clinic built dedicated conversation flows for each stage of the patient journey: pre-appointment consultations, follow-up reminders, medication reminders, and post-operative guidance. Jinmi AI stores conversation history and personalises messaging — patients feel individually cared for at every touchpoint.
  </Step>
  <Step title="CRM synchronisation and real-time reporting">
    The system connected directly to the clinic's CRM, automatically creating appointments and storing patient records. Performance dashboards for each channel and each staff member updated in real-time, enabling the management team to make accurate, data-driven operational decisions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
