Customers message you on Facebook at midnight, Zalo in the morning, and your website all afternoon. Jinmi Omnichannel brings every channel into a single inbox and deploys an AI chatbot that consults, answers, and closes sales around the clock — turning conversations into revenue even while you sleep.

## What Omnichannel solves

Running a business across multiple channels creates real problems: messages get missed, staff get overwhelmed, and customers leave when they don't get a fast answer. Jinmi Omnichannel addresses all of this in one platform.

| Problem | Solution |
| --- | --- |
| Messages scattered across Facebook, Zalo, Email, Telegram, and your website | One unified inbox for every channel |
| Customers abandoning orders due to slow replies | AI chatbot responds instantly, 24/7 |
| No visibility into which channels perform best | Analytics dashboard with per-channel metrics |
| High staffing cost for repetitive support tasks | Automation handles routine questions and follow-ups |
| Staff replying inconsistently across teams | Shared inbox with assignments, tags, and templates |

## Three pillars of Omnichannel

### Communication & customer support

Turn every online channel into a 24/7 concierge. Live Chat, Omnichannel Inbox, and AI Chatbot ensure instant replies without overloading your team.

**Key features:** Website Live Chat, Omnichannel Inbox, AI Chatbot 24/7, Automations

### Convenience & flexibility

Manage and respond anywhere with the mobile app. All channel messages land in a single app so you never miss a customer, whether you're at your desk or on the go.

**Key features:** Mobile Apps (iOS & Android), Integrations, Pre-Chat Form, Help Center

### Analytics & optimization

Move from gut-feel to data-driven decisions. Visualize conversations, agent performance, and channel comparisons to optimize cost, conversion rate, and customer experience.

**Key features:** Analytics & Reporting, Speed & Productivity, Extended Multi-Channel Connectivity
