---
title: "How to integrate your website"
description: "Add a Jinmi live chat widget to your website and route visitor messages into the Omnichannel inbox. Step-by-step integration instructions included."
---

Step 1: Select **Settings** => click **Inbox** => **Add channel connection**

\

Step 2: Select **Website** => enter the information => click **Create inbox**

\

Step 3: Select the support agents for the fanpage => click **Add agents**

\
\
\
\

Step 4: A successful connection notification will appear and you can go to the conversation
