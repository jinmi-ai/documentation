---
title: "How to integrate a Facebook Fanpage"
description: "Connect your Facebook Fanpage to Jinmi Omnichannel so Messenger conversations appear in your unified inbox. Follow this step-by-step setup guide."
---

Step 1: Select **Settings** => click **Inbox** => **Add channel connection**

Step 2: Select **Facebook** and click **Continue with facebook**

Step 3: Log in to Facebook to connect

Step 4: Connect

\

Step 5: Select the page you want to connect => Click **Create inbox**

Step 6: Select the support agents for the fanpage => click **Add agents**

Step 7: After a successful connection, the screen will display a notification and a QR code to open the inbox on the fanpage
